With modular programming, concerns are separated such that modules perform logically discrete functions, interacting through well-defined interfaces.

👍 Modular designed systems, if built correctly, are far more reusable than a traditional monolithic design, since all (or many) of these modules may then be reused.

👍 A modularized software project will be more easily assembled by large teams, since no team members are creating the whole system, or even need to know about the system as a whole. They can focus just on the assigned smaller task.
